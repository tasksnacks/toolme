What is Annualized ROI?

Earning 20% in one year is very different from earning 20% over ten years. Annualized ROI shows you the yearly efficiency rate so you can compare investments of different lengths fairly.

Understanding ROI

ROI (Return on Investment) answers the simple question: "For every dollar I put in, how much did I get back?" It is the most common metric for evaluating financial success.

Why use this tool?

Unlike simple calculators, we let you input specific dates to handle real-world scenarios where investments don't fit perfectly into "1 year" blocks.
